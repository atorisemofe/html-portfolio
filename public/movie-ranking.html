Greatest Anime Series of All Time by Oreo

My top 3 Anime Series

Naruto

This is about a young ninja who's goal is to become the Hokage of his village. The Hokage is considered the stongest Ninja in the village, most loved and acknowleged.

Hunter x Hunter

This is about a boy on a journey to get his Hunters license and become as great as his legendary hunter father.

Attack on Titan

This is the story of a young boy who's life comes crashing down when Titans invade his hometown, killing his mother and friends. His livelihood is stolen from him and he joins the scout regiment to go beyond the walls and kill titans.
